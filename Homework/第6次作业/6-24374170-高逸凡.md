## 五次Hermite插值多项式（基函数法）

给定节点数据：

| $x$ | 0 | 1 | 2 |
|------|---|---|---|
| $f(x)$ | 0 | 2 | 1 |
| $f'(x)$ | 1 | 0 | -2 |

构造五次Hermite插值多项式 $H_5(x)$ 满足
$H_5(x_i)=f(x_i),\ H_5'(x_i)=f'(x_i),\ i=0,1,2.$

### 1. 拉格朗日基函数

$$
l_0(x)=\frac{(x-1)(x-2)}{(0-1)(0-2)}=\frac{1}{2}(x^2-3x+2),
$$

$$
l_1(x)=\frac{(x-0)(x-2)}{(1-0)(1-2)}=-x(x-2)=-x^2+2x,
$$

$$
l_2(x)=\frac{(x-0)(x-1)}{(2-0)(2-1)}=\frac{1}{2}x(x-1).
$$

### 2. Hermite基函数

$$
h_i(x)=\left[1-2(x-x_i)\sum_{\substack{j=0\\j\neq i}}^{2}\frac{1}{x_i-x_j}\right]\bigl[l_i(x)\bigr]^2,\qquad
g_i(x)=(x-x_i)\bigl[l_i(x)\bigr]^2.
$$


- 对于 $i=0$：
  $\displaystyle\sum_{j\neq0}\frac{1}{x_0-x_j}=\frac{1}{0-1}+\frac{1}{0-2}=-1-\frac12=-\frac32$，
  故 $1-2(x-x_0)\cdot\left(-\frac32\right)=1+3x$.

- 对于 $i=1$：
  $\displaystyle\sum_{j\neq1}\frac{1}{x_1-x_j}=\frac{1}{1-0}+\frac{1}{1-2}=1-1=0$，
  故 $1-2(x-x_1)\cdot0=1$.

- 对于 $i=2$：
  $\displaystyle\sum_{j\neq2}\frac{1}{x_2-x_j}=\frac{1}{2-0}+\frac{1}{2-1}=\frac12+1=\frac32$，
  故 $1-2(x-x_2)\cdot\frac32=1-3(x-2)=7-3x$.

于是得到Hermite基函数：

$$
\begin{aligned}
h_0(x)&=(1+3x)\bigl[l_0(x)\bigr]^2=(1+3x)\cdot\frac{1}{4}(x^2-3x+2)^2,\\
g_0(x)&=(x-0)\bigl[l_0(x)\bigr]^2=x\cdot\frac{1}{4}(x^2-3x+2)^2,\\[4pt]
h_1(x)&=1\cdot\bigl[l_1(x)\bigr]^2=x^2(x-2)^2,\\
g_1(x)&=(x-1)\bigl[l_1(x)\bigr]^2=(x-1)x^2(x-2)^2,\\[4pt]
h_2(x)&=(7-3x)\bigl[l_2(x)\bigr]^2=(7-3x)\cdot\frac{1}{4}x^2(x-1)^2,\\
g_2(x)&=(x-2)\bigl[l_2(x)\bigr]^2=(x-2)\cdot\frac{1}{4}x^2(x-1)^2.
\end{aligned}
$$

### 3. 插值多项式

$$
\begin{aligned}
H_5(x)&=\sum_{i=0}^{2}f(x_i)h_i(x)+\sum_{i=0}^{2}f'(x_i)g_i(x)\\
&=0\cdot h_0(x)+2\cdot h_1(x)+1\cdot h_2(x)+1\cdot g_0(x)+0\cdot g_1(x)+(-2)\cdot g_2(x)\\
&=2x^2(x-2)^2+\frac{7-3x}{4}x^2(x-1)^2+\frac{x}{4}(x^2-3x+2)^2-\frac{1}{2}x^2(x-1)^2(x-2).
\end{aligned}
$$

合并得：

$$
H_5(x)=2x^2(x-2)^2+\frac{11-5x}{4}x^2(x-1)^2+\frac{x}{4}(x-1)^2(x-2)^2.
$$

$$
\begin{aligned}
2x^2(x-2)^2 &= 2x^4-8x^3+8x^2,\\
\frac{11-5x}{4}x^2(x-1)^2 &= -\frac{5}{4}x^5+\frac{21}{4}x^4-\frac{27}{4}x^3+\frac{11}{4}x^2,\\
\frac{x}{4}(x-1)^2(x-2)^2 &= \frac{1}{4}x^5-\frac{3}{2}x^4+\frac{13}{4}x^3-3x^2+x.
\end{aligned}
$$

相加得：

$$
H_5(x)=-x^5+\frac{23}{4}x^4-\frac{23}{2}x^3+\frac{31}{4}x^2+x.
$$

### 4. 计算 $H_5(1.5)$

$$
\begin{aligned}
H_5(1.5)&=-(1.5)^5+\frac{23}{4}(1.5)^4-\frac{23}{2}(1.5)^3+\frac{31}{4}(1.5)^2+1.5\\
&=-7.59375+5.75\times5.0625-11.5\times3.375+7.75\times2.25+1.5\\
&=-7.59375+29.109375-38.8125+17.4375+1.5\\
&=1.640625.
\end{aligned}
$$

因此，五次Hermite插值多项式为 $H_5(x)=-x^5+\dfrac{23}{4}x^4-\dfrac{23}{2}x^3+\dfrac{31}{4}x^2+x$，且 $H_5(1.5)=1.640625$。
